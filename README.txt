InviBiz25 v1.0 — Beta Release
==============================
Thank you for testing InviBiz25!

GETTING STARTED
---------------
1. Double-click InviBiz25.exe to launch
2. On first run, go to Settings and fill in your
   business name, address, and email
3. These details will appear on every invoice PDF

YOUR DATA
---------
All your data is stored locally in invoicer.db,
saved right next to InviBiz25.exe. Nothing is
sent to the internet. To reset the app completely,
just delete invoicer.db.

FEATURES IN v1.0
----------------
- Invoice creation with live totals, tax %, discount & shipping
- PDF export with your logo and business info
- Client & CRM manager with call notes and follow-ups
- Dashboard with recent invoices and upcoming follow-ups
- Business settings including e-transfer email

REPORTING BUGS
--------------
Please note what you were doing when something
went wrong and send it back to us. Screenshots help!

Built with Python + CustomTkinter.
