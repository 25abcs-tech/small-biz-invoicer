================================================
  InvoBiz v1.0 Beta
  Invoicing and Business Management made Easy
================================================

Thank you for trying InvoBiz! This is a beta
release — your feedback helps shape the final
version. Please report any bugs or suggestions
to the contact details at the bottom.


------------------------------------------------
  GETTING STARTED
------------------------------------------------

1. Double-click InvoBiz.exe to launch
2. If Windows shows a security warning, click
   "More info" then "Run anyway" — this is normal
   for new software without a code signing cert
3. On first launch, go to Settings and fill in
   your business name, address, and email
4. These details appear on every invoice PDF


------------------------------------------------
  WHAT'S IN v1.0 BETA
------------------------------------------------

INVOICING
  - Create invoices with live totals
  - Tax (%), discounts, and shipping support
  - Export professional PDF invoices
  - Your logo and business info on every PDF
  - Invoice history with search and year filter

CLIENTS & CRM
  - Full client manager with contact details
  - Call notes and follow-up tracking
  - Purchase history per client
  - Prev / Next navigation between clients

SALES PIPELINE
  - 5-stage pipeline: Cold Lead to Onboarded
  - Kanban-style pipeline board view
  - Territory view grouped by province/state
  - Pipeline stage badges on client cards

FULFILMENT TRACKER
  - Track orders from placed to delivered
  - 4-stage progress: Ordered, Packing,
    Shipped, Delivered
  - Overdue alerts (3 days packing,
    5 days shipped)
  - One-click carrier tracking links
  - Supports Canada Post, UPS, FedEx,
    Purolator, DHL + add your own carriers

DASHBOARD
  - Stats: invoices, revenue, follow-ups
  - Yearly revenue bar chart
  - Quick invoice search
  - Period reset for new quarter/year tracking


------------------------------------------------
  YOUR DATA
------------------------------------------------

All data is stored locally in invoicer.db,
saved right next to InvoBiz.exe on your
computer. Nothing is sent to the internet.

To back up your data: copy invoicer.db
To reset the app:     delete invoicer.db
To move computers:    copy both InvoBiz.exe
                      and invoicer.db


------------------------------------------------
  KNOWN BETA LIMITATIONS
